Local Run

how it works

Cold weather running can be difficult. One look at the
temperature and your motivation to run can be instantly
dissolved. This application’s goal is to help you stay
motivated. By allowing Google’s Geolocation software to
determine your location, this software will let you know
immediately what gear to wear on your workout. All without
having to view the temperature! Enough knowledge to get
you running without being discouraged by the temperature.
